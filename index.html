Recipe #1 Upma

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 3
  • Upma is an indian subcontinent dish.
  • It take very less time to cook. So it can be made in breakfast.
  • It is also called Uppittu and concrete.
  • You may also enjoy it with coconut cutney.
Ingredients Quantity
Samolina 1 cup
Finely choped onion 1 medium sized
Musturd seed 1 tsp
curry leaves 12-15
roasted peanut 1 tblsp
Oil 1 tblsp
Green chille(chopped) 1
Salt 1 tsp

Step 1: Dry roast the samolina for 5-6 min, till golden brown and set aside.
Step 2: In a deep pan, pour oil, when the oil heats, put Musturd seed, after 2-3 seconds, put in the onion.
Step 3: cook the onion till it turn pink in color.
Step 4:Add in green chille, peanut and curry leaves
Step 5: Add 1 and a half cup water and as water start to boil, Add in the samolina, slowly while continuous stirring it.
Step 4: let it cook for 1 minute and then turn of the stove. Your Umpa is ready enjoy!

Recipe #2 Poha

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 3
  • Poha is a breakfast indian subcontinent dish.
  • It is easy to cook.
  • In some parts of india it is garnished on top with indian snacks mix(namkeen)
  • Also be made with rolled oats if flattened rice is unavailable or to make it healthier.
Ingredients Quantity
Flattened rice flakes(poha) 1 cup
Finely choped onion 1 medium sized
Musturd seed 1 tsp
curry leaves 12-15
roasted peanut 1 tblsp
Oil 1-2 tblsp
Green chille(chopped) 1
Turmeric powder 1/3 tsp
Lemon juice 2 tblsp
Salt 1 tsp
Coriander leaves(chopped) 3 tblsp (optional)

Step 1: Wash 1 cup flattened rice flakes and soak for 3 seconds then drain the water quickly or it will become mushy. And set aside.
Step 2: In a deep pan, pour oil, when the oil heats, put Musturd seed, after 2-3 seconds, put in the onion.
Step 3: cook the onion till it turn pink in color.
Step 4:Add in green chille, peanut, curry leaves and turmeric.
Step 5: Now add in the rice flakes.
Step 4: Stir and mix with light hands. Add in the lemon juice, mix lighly.
Step 5:Serve and enjoy your poha!

Recipe #3 Cheela

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4
  • Pancakes prepared using a north Indian cooking style are known as cheela
  • Poha is a indian subcontinent breakfast dish.
  • It is easy to cook.
Ingredients Quantity
Gram flour (Besan) 1 cup
Finely choped onion 1 medium sized
Carom seeds 1/2 tsp
Turmeric 1/2 tsp
Canenne powder 1/2 tsp
Green chille(chopped) 1
Coriander leaves(chopped) 1-2 tblsp
Salt 1 tsp

Step 1: In a bowl take onion,Turmeric,carom seeds and gram flour and mix well.
Step 2: Add 1/2 cup water slowly making a slight thick paste.
Step 3: Mix well, so that there is no lumps, and set aside
Step 4:Take a flat pan
Step 5: Pour little oil and spread well over the pan surface.
Step 4: Pour laddel full of batter and spread it to make 6-8 inches in size.
Step 5:cook for 5-6 minutes, till it turns light brown by pouring some oil on the sides.
Step 5:As the sides starts coming off ,flip on the other side, cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step 4: Repeat for rest of the batter.
Step 4: You can enjoy it with chutney.
